PICT B.E. Course Structure

An overview of the subjects for each year of the Bachelor of Engineering program.

First Year (FE)

  • Engineering Mathematics I: Foundational calculus and algebra.
  • Basic Electrical Engineering: Introduction to circuits and systems.
  • Programming and Problem Solving: Fundamentals of programming using C.

Second Year (SE)

  • Data Structures and Algorithms: Core concepts of organizing data.
  • Computer Organization and Architecture: Understanding computer hardware.
  • Digital Electronics and Logic Design: Basics of digital circuits.

Third Year (TE)

  • Database Management Systems: Theory and application of databases.
  • Theory of Computation: Formal languages and automata theory.
  • Software Engineering: Principles of software development lifecycle.

Fourth Year (BE)

  • Artificial Intelligence: Introduction to AI concepts and algorithms.
  • Cloud Computing: Fundamentals of cloud platforms and services.
  • Elective I: Specialization course.
